For hosptials, polygons are twice as nice!

Welcome to our Targeted Editing series where today you can eat a peppermint, or make OpenStreetMap data excellent! (both take about the same amount of time)

  1. How can hospital polygons be used?

    1. Help with map rendering! No, this doesn't mean cook the fat out of it, this means help with how things are cartographically displayed on the map. A map label is great to have, but a polygon is even better!

    2. To add a polygon for a hospital that is only represented by a point, hover over one of the bright blue highlighted hospital points to bring up an info bubble with links to editing tools that can be used to add features!

        3. See the wiki pages for amenity=hospital for lots of details and a great example.
